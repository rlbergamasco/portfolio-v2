Album Redesign

Project for MEJO 482: Media Design - January 2021

This project involved redesigning an album cover (right) in the style of Blue Note Records and adapting the design into a promotional poster (bottom left) and Spotify canvas artwork (bottom right). I chose Miley Cyrus's 2020 album Plastic Hearts for the redesign.

The goal of the project was to create a cohesive design across three formats that remains connected to the artist's own visual style while emulating Blue Note Records' distinct style. This design was inspired by the albums Newk's Time by Sonny Rollins, Una Mas by Kenny Dorham, and Chant by Donald Byrd
