Editorial Illustration

Project for MEJO 482: Media Design - February 2021

Editorial illustration for a fictional story about a Top Secret project the United States government recently launched that integrates all of its military intelligence-gathering efforts into a state-of-the-art computer system called Hexion. This system links all six branches of the nation’s military under a single, massive satellite network that can locate, track, and eavesdrop on the conversations of individuals who may pose a threat to American national security anywhere on the globe. Hexion will not only track individuals’ movements and communications but will use highly-classified artificial intelligence software to build psychological profiles of these individuals and predict their actions and movements.
