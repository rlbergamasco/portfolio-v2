That Costs What?

Project made at queer_hack hackathon - December 2020

A trivia game that compares U.S. Healthcare prices with prices in other countries. Responsively designed for mobile and desktop. This project was made in 24 hours with Rebecca Whitten and Mikenna Breen using React.js. None of us had any previous experience with React.js. View the project.
